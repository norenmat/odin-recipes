Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a household name in the west african dishes. It is known that the Nigerian jollof rice is the best in the world but so many other african countries are trying to catch up with the competition of this amazing delicacy

Ingredients

There are different ingredients used in the preparation of jollof rice but the most common and important ones are listed below:

  • Rice
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Sauce
  • Water

Step: Preparation

  1. Wash the rice and put in a pot
  2. Put water in the pot and boil for 10 minutes
  3. Wash the pepper and blend
  4. Put the vegetable oil in a pot and heat for 5 minutes
  5. Pour the blended pepper into the pot and fry for 10 minutes
  6. Pour the sauce into the pot and stir for 5 minutes
  7. Pour the boiled rice into the pot and stir for 5 minutes
  8. Allow to cook for 10 minutes
  9. Remove from heat and serve
