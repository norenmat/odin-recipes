Ikokore

Description

Ikorore is a household name among the Ijebu people of southwest Nigeria. It gives the best feeling to the local people and also to the new visitors an amazing experience

Ingredients

There are different ingredients used in the preparation of Ikorore but the most common and important ones are listed below:

  • Water yam
  • Pepper
  • Palm Oil
  • Sauce
  • Water

Step: Preparation

  1. Wash the water yam and peel the back
  2. Grate the water yam
  3. Wash the pepper and blend
  4. Put the palm oil in a pot and heat for 5 minutes
  5. Pour the blended pepper into the pot and fry for 10 minutes
  6. Pour the sauce into the pot and stir for 5 minutes
  7. Pour the grated water yam into the pot and stir for 5 minutes
  8. Allow to cook for 10 minutes
  9. Remove from heat and serve
